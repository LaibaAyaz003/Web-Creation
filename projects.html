Chic Couture: Fashion E-Commerce Website

I developed **Chic Couture**, a vibrant and user-friendly e-commerce platform designed for fashion boutiques. The website features a sleek interface, with dynamic product pages, and integrated shopping cart functionality. I focused on responsive design, ensuring the site looks fabulous across devices. With smooth transitions and pastel-colored aesthetics, it gives users a seamless, enjoyable shopping experience. This project challenged me to merge style with functionality and taught me the intricacies of front-end and back-end integration.

Blossom: Wellness & Self-Care App

**Blossom** is a self-care mobile app designed to help users track their mental and physical wellness. It includes features such as daily mood tracking, goal setting, and personalized self-care suggestions. I designed the app with a soft color palette and a calming UI to provide a relaxing user experience. Working on Blossom helped me enhance my mobile app development skills, with a focus on user experience, API integration, and data security.
